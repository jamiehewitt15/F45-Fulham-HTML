F45 TOWER BRIDGE STUDIO IS NOW CLOSED BUT CONTINUE WITH AT-HOME WORKOUTS WITH US
OUR UPDATED ACTION PLAN IN-STUDIO
As the situation on COVID-19 evolves, we are working hard behind the scenes to provide solutions in order to keep members safe, while still providing the best possible workout. Changes have been made to the timing of this week's sessions from Tuesday, 17th March onwards. If you or any of your household have been advised to self quarantine, please stay away from the studio and Whatsapp us ASAP 07479013235.
SMALLER CLASSES
Reducing class sizes, to allow for social distancing measures between stations. Increase the time between classes so with your help, our team can properly disinfect and sanitize the studio and equipment before the next class.
EQUIPMENT CHANGES
Sandbags/core bags/activation bands excluded due to extra sanitising measures and person-to-person contact. In each workout we have removed as much movement as possible, therefore all working sets will be completed at one station before moving.
TIME CHANGES
The last rest interval of each set has been increased to allow for proper sanitisation at each station before moving. At this stage, the shortest rest/cleaning period is 45 seconds (Panthers) and most will increase to 60 seconds or more.
FLUID CLASS CHANGES
We will monitor the situation and may make the decision to move to body weight classes only (Gravity). We have already taken the warm-up off the floor and leading an active warm down standing up where time allows.
F45 ETIQUETTE TO STAYING SAFE IN-STUDIO
KNOWLEDGE IS POWER
Information is changing quickly. Stay informed on the facts. Here are some useful resources for everyone:
Public Health England (PHE)
World Health Organization
BEST DEFENSE IS GOOD OFFENCE
Our studio is stocked with hand sanitiser, disinfectant spray, wipes and tissues. Use them during, before and after. We have extra supplies and bottles placed around the studio.
WASH YOUR HANDS
Good hygiene is important. Please wash your hands for at least 20 seconds when you arrive at the studio. 20 seconds of scrubbing bubbles is nothing compared to 45 seconds on the rower.
USE OUR TOWELS
Sweat is great, but not when someone else has to work out in it. We provide towels but bring your own if you prefer. Grab one before class and use it on all equipment as you move from station to station so your teammates feel comfortable and happy during their workouts, too.
TOE TAPS OR CLAP, NO HAND SLAPS
We love you, but for the time being, we will be replacing them with toe taps of affection and a Group Clap. Then go to the changerooms and wash your hands. It's important to avoid touching your eyes, nose, mouth and others where possible.
SNEEZE N WEEZE, STAY AWAY PLEASE
If you're sick or have any flu like symptoms, please don't come to the studio. Follow Public Health England Guidance and Advice and self isolate. We promise, a little delay won't keep you from achieving your fitness goals! Or follow our at home solution. We have are options!
REMINDER ADDITIONAL MEASURES IN-STUDIO
Alongside our at-home solution, we want to minimise disruption to your fitness routine. Most important do what is comfortable for you. It's a confusing time. Lots of advisory measures however no official directives or lock-downs for business. For those that are enjoying the continued routine here's a reminder of our additional in-studio measures.
There are now multiple disinfectant spray bottles with white towels around the studio at exercise stations and ask every member to help out with a quick spray and wipe at every station after each set.
Hand Sanitiser is at the front door however nothing is more effective than washing your hands thoroughly with soap and hot water before and after class in the changerooms.
Daily Deep cleaning will continue with our professional cleaning company to ensure the studio is maintained to the highest level.
We disinfect and wipe down all equipment before and after each class with anti-bacterial solution.
On top of deep cleaning, we're working with our cleaning partner to pay additional attention to high touch and key areas in the studio.
Class Time Schedule as normal (exception of NO 18:50 class) 7 days a week.
Top taps, Elbow taps are optional. Group Clap is the Go!
Protein shake service will stop for now.
Watch out for modified warm ups from the screens. We'll be minimizing the time spent on the floor.
We wash and dry our sweat and shower towels in 60° hot water with laundry detergent however please bring your own towel if you prefer.
Class start times may be slightly delayed if necessary to allow for these extra measures.
We have stopped leading the guided warm-down between classes to allow time to disinfect spray and wipe down equipment to the best of our ability. Feel free to self-stretch or warm down at this time. Where time permits, we will be leading an active warm-down standing up.
