If You Can't Make the Class...
We kindly ask for your consideration and cancel your booking no less than 3 hours in advance so that wait listed members can join the session in good time. Don't forget, as penalty fees apply for 'no shows' and 'late cancellation'. If you choose to be wait listed you will automatically be added to the class and notified by email. Please read our Class Booking Rules you have accepted in our terms before booking a class. We operate a ZERO GRACE policy on wait-listed classes.
REVIEW CLASS BOOKING RULES
Arrival 'Check-In' to Class
On arrival, someone may greet you at reception but members must self-check-in on the iPad which is located by the reception counter - if you don't it may be counted as a 'no show' or 'late cancellation'. Whilst waiting for the class to begin, feel free to motivate other members, share your workout goals and training progress. If it's your first class, give a quick hi to the trainers and watch the flow of the studio during a class.
Get Prepared
We are a sustainable studio. Please bring your own refillable water bottle or purchase an re-fillable water bottle available at reception counter. We provide chilled 'Eau De Vie' filtered water taps. Store your belongings and non valuables in the open storage cubes in reception. There are a limited number of secure lockers available in the change rooms for valuables.
Pre Exercise Form & Profile Pic
If you haven't already when registering your Fulham account, we may ask you to agree to a pre-exercise form before your first work out. We may also request a quick headshot pic in studio for your profile account (only for our purposes).
Before Training
Please remain within the reception area or in the covered outdoor waiting area until the warm up for your class begins. Be aware of the exiting class by making way to collect their belongings. Be on time, there is a 5 minute warm-up during which you will be allocated a station number which you don't want to miss.
Training & Workout
If it's your first class, don't be shy to tell us. If you have any injuries, please volunteer it to the trainers for alternative exercises. If at any point in the class you are unsure of anything please ask. If you feel faint, stop exercising immediately and tell one of the trainers. Stay hydrated.
After Class
At the end of the class, we run a 5 min warm down, however if you are in a hurry please feel free to shoot off. We provide shampoo, conditioner, lotion and body wash, hair dryers and shower towels. Please don't forget to drop used towels in the towel bin on exit.
Pricing and Memberships
We offer a range of pricing options to suit your requirements. If you are in studio and would like to discuss membership talk to any of the team. Concentrating on delivering great classes is our main priority so in peak times, we may request you send an email or arrange a suitable time to chat through the options. Please note, only 'Class Passes' (casual) are visible on the APP, for Current 'Membership Options' (longer term) and 'Class Passes' visit the desktop FFFTB Online Store.

F45 TB CLASS BOOKING RULES
We would like everyone to achieve their fitness goals. So that every member has the opportunity to train at their desired time and members on the wait-list can be moved onto class, we discourage no-shows and late cancellations with penalties as part of our agreed terms in purchasing services from F45 Fulham. A copy of these terms can also be found at http://FFFTB.co.uk/memberspage/#classbookingrules

Booking Rules

As a part of our community, we kindly request you respect all members by following our class booking rules:

• You agree to 'OPT IN' for email notifications for 'Reminders & Schedule Changes' in your F45 Fulham online profile account.
• You must cancel outside of the late cancellation window to avoid penalty fees.
• If you are booked into a class and do not show a 'No Show Fee' applies.
• It is a member's responsibility to Check-In on the studio iPad upon arrival to class or risk a 'No Show' or 'Late Cancellation' automatically being registered.
• 'No Show' or 'Late Cancellation' on an Introductory Offer or Experience your pass may be subject to immediate cancellation.
• 'Wait List' is an opt-in automated feature, you will automatically be added to the class as soon as a place becomes available and notified by email.
• It is a member's responsibility to cancel out of any alternative class booked if you are automatically moved onto an alternative wait-listed class.
• You cannot wait list on multiple classes on the same day, F45 Fulham reserve the right to cancel your wait list on multiple wait-listed classes.
• F45 Fulham cannot guarantee a place if you choose to 'Show Up' for a class without making a confirmed booking and you may be turned away.
• Penalty fees are automated, you agree to self check-in on the member's iPad upon arrival.
• F45 Fulham reserve the right to charge penalty fees at their discretion irrespective of notice given within the late cancellation window.
• There is a ZERO grace policy on peak-hour classes.
• Any 'Fee' is automatically taken via D/D or card on file.

Penalty Fees
Late Cancellation Fee - Morning & Weekend Classes
• 6 hour cancellation window prior to the published start time.
• We suggest you cancel 'Before-You-Go-To-Bed'.
• £5 Late Cancel Fee.

Late Cancellation Fee - Lunch & Evening Classes
• 1.5 hour cancellation window prior to the published start time.
• £5 Late Cancel Fee.

No-Show Fee (All Classes)
• Booked but you do not attend the class.
• £15 No Show Fee.

Wait-Lists: if you choose to enroll, it's your responsibility to:
• Check if you've been added to the class after the Late Cancellation window closes.
• Cancel out of any alternative classes you may have booked and no longer want to attend.
• Penalties still apply to all bookings outside the cancellation window.
• If you're added to a class after the 'Late Cancellation' window closes and don't make it – you won't be liable for penalty fee.
